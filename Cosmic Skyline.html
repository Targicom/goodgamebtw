This is the tutorial panel, the place where you get all the necessary information on how to play the game or maneuver around the menus.

MENUS:

Change your name by pressing the Change Name button. Pretty self-explanatory. Press the Deck maker button in which you create your deck.

DECK MAKER:

Search or type the names of specific cards in the three boxes representing the types. Orange (Terra) Violet Red (Pyro) Blue (Hydro) . You can only fill one box at a time and you can view the card's properties on the description tab, to the right of the 3 boxes. Below the 3 boxes, you can view your deck which starts out as empty. Once you decide on what card you want simply press Add Card.

In order to remove a card, simply press the card from your deck that you want to remove and click "Remove Card".

By the way, you can add Terra or any other type of cards from another box that's not their dedicated "home". It's just that it's not recommended, since there's no autocomplete.

TYPES:

1. Terra is the first type, which is the simplest one and based on basic attacks combined with the Armor property.

2. Pyro is the most powerful of the types, based on raw damage and debuffs. Perfect for an aggresive playstyle.

3. Hydro is the most defensive of the three, based on healing,defense and health in general. This type is recommended for the intrigued player, since it's the most interesting one.

STATUSES:

1. Overgrown status increases armor bonuses that cards give. For example, a card gives you 1 armor. That 1 armor becomes 2. Also damage taken from Pyro cards is boosted by 10% and damage from Hydro cards is decreased by 10%.

2. Overheated status increases Pyro based damage by 20%, but every single round 5 hp is lost.

3. Cleansed status increases all healing by 20% and reduces all damage by 10%.

GAMEPLAY:

For starters, you have a hand of 6 cards. Each round your deck's cards randomly get put on your hand, meaning for the most part you'll be getting different cards each round.

There are 2 options, FIGHT and PASS. FIGHT brings up your moves in the bottom part of your screen, as well as the Active Spell Box (The red box) and your health and armor and essence in a dark-gray colored box.

ACTIVE SPELL:

You might have seen, "Takes active spell slot" in some cards. The Active Spell is a type of spell that takes some turns to take effect OR has an effect every turn, but obviously it lasts a specific amount of turns. If there's an active spell active, then it's displayed in the red box on the bottom left corner. If you're on mobile that box is most likely somewhere else.

MECHANICS:

Believe it or not Armor is not related to actual damage reduction. It's instead a stat that some cards take advantage of. Essence is used to, well, use moves.There's not much to it, but every round you get 1 essence . Same goes for the opponent.

You and your opponents have a Speed property, which determines who goes first. In all of the fights you are faster than your enemies. Your speed is 1, theirs is 0. There's a card that swaps your speed with the opponent's. Better watch out for that!

In order to determine who uses their move first, your speed or your selected move's priority has to be higher. Otherwise the opponent uses their move first.

ENTERING FIGHT:

If you press PASS, then the fight starts with you, well, passing the round. Don't worry you'll still get that 1 essence at the end of the round. If you press FIGHT and press on the move that you want,you'll notice a small info box at the left of your screen. If you can't read, because the font is too small, simply press the "I" button to zoom in. In order to start the fight press the green button labeled "CONFIRM ACTION" at the top left corner of the screen.

If you're unsure about how tough things will be, well i can assure you as long as you play smart and make any deck you will most likely win.

That's basically it! After beating the boss once, you can move to the other bosses, as well as add 5 hp to your total or if you have beaten the 2nd boss, you gain 1 attack. Have fun with the game!
